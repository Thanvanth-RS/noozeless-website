Designed to Wake You Up

NoozeLess helps you build better morning habits with these key features

No Snooze Alarm

Eliminate the temptation to snooze. Once your alarm goes off, you must complete a simple challenge to stop it.

Wake-Up Challenges

Solve quick math problems, memory games, or physical movement checks to dismiss the alarm and fully wake up.

Offline Reliability

Works completely offline. No internet connection required for alarms to ring or challenges to function.

Clean & Simple Interface

Intuitive design with minimal distractions. Set alarms quickly and manage your wake-up schedule effortlessly.

How NoozeLess Works

Three simple steps to transform your mornings

1

Set Your Alarm

Choose your wake-up time and customize your wake-up challenge difficulty level.

2

Alarm Rings On Time

Your alarm will sound at the exact time you set, with no option to snooze or dismiss easily.

3

Complete a Challenge to Stop

Solve a simple mental or physical challenge to stop the alarm, ensuring you're fully awake.
