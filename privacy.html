NoozeLess Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

NoozeLess is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

NoozeLess does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. The app is designed to function entirely on your device without transmitting any information to external servers.

What we don't collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or unique IDs
  • Location data or GPS information
  • Contact lists or phone numbers
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Financial or payment information

Third-Party Services

NoozeLess does not use any third-party services that collect user data. The app operates independently without integrating with advertising networks, analytics services, or social media platforms.

Since no data is collected, there is no data to share with third parties under any circumstances.

Device Permissions

NoozeLess may request certain permissions to provide core functionality:

Alarm Permission

Required to set alarms and wake you up at the scheduled time.

Audio/Vibration

Needed to play alarm sounds and vibrate your device when alarms trigger.

Do Not Disturb Access

Optional permission to ensure alarms sound even when Do Not Disturb mode is active.

All permissions are used only for their intended functionality within the app and are not used to collect or transmit data.

Children's Privacy

NoozeLess is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Since the app does not collect any data, there are no special considerations needed for children's privacy. Parents can feel confident allowing their children to use NoozeLess.

Data Storage

All data created by NoozeLess (such as alarm settings and preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data is not backed up to any cloud services or external servers.

If you uninstall NoozeLess, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted from your device.

Questions & Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or NoozeLess's privacy practices, please contact us at:

thanvanthrs@gmail.com

We will respond to legitimate inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Continued use of NoozeLess after any modifications to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide by the updated policy.

Privacy Summary

NoozeLess is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app functions entirely on your device, collects no data, and shares nothing with anyone. Your wake-up routine remains your private business.
